When to use Vue.js depends on your project's requirements. Consider Vue.js when: You need to create interactive and responsive user interfaces. Your team prefers a framework that's easy to learn and work with. You want to build a single-page application (SPA) or need dynamic UI components within a larger application. You value a well-documented and actively maintained framework. You're working on a project where the simplicity and flexibility of Vue.js align with your goals. However, remember that the choice of a framework ultimately depends on your project's specific needs and your team's expertise. Other popular options like React and Angular are also worth considering, depending on the situation.
